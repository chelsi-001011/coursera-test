Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken or Makhan murg (Hindi: मुर्ग़ मक्खनी) (pronounced [mʊrg ˈmək.kʰə.niː]) is a dish, originating in the Indian subcontinent, of chicken in a mildly spiced tomato sauce.(Our speciality)

Chhole Bhature

Chole Bhature is a dish originated initially from eastern Uttar Pradesh in the northern part of the Indian Subcontinent. Now this dish is famous invariably in north while having huge following in Punjab.

Dosa

A Dosa is a cooked flat thin layered rice batter, originating from the South India, made from a fermented batter. It is somewhat similar to a crepe in appearance. They are typically served with a vegetarian side dish which varies according to regional and personal preferences.
